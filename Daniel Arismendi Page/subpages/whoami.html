My name is Daniel. I come from a family mainly composed of my mom and my grandmother. My dad was present in my life and was essential, but he didn’t live with me. We shared good moments, and he is fundamental in shaping who I am now. However, I am who I am because of my mom and my grandmother.

I studied my whole life in public institutions—public school, public university. In this way, I was able to obtain my degree in political science and later specialize in photography and economics.

At the university, I met my now wife, with whom I live in Chicago. We are together facing the challenge of living in a new place and opening up paths for our family and future generations.
